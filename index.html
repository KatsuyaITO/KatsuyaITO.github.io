Self-Introduction

Born in 1994 in Kobe/Akashi, I graduated from the University of Tokyo with a degree in Mathematics and a Master’s in Economics. After starting my career in 2019, I worked as an engineer at PFN, an unicorn AI startup, and gained experience in new business development utilizing AI at Mitsui & Co., Ltd., a major trading company. Recently, I founded INDX, a company dedicated to organising and analysing unstructured data from Japanese companies to unlock new value and insights.
